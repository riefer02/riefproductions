Music and Audio Services

I’m a music producer, audio engineer, and sounder designer. I produce music from my studio based in Austin, Texas. I've produced albums for singer songwriters, rock stars, and indie artists. I do contract work for businesses that need professional audio editing and mixing for their digital online platforms. I design video game music for game designers. I also produce videos for musicians, product promotions, and social media. Over the past decade, I’ve taken my music and production skills from an organized hobby to a profession. My intent is to help you create audio that stands out and speaks to your listeners.

– Andrew Riefenstahl
